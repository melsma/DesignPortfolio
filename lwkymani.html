'LWKY' is a manifesto made for designers in the future. It emphasizes the importance of responsible and ethical design in the context of modern commerce and advertising.

This manifesto is a call for designers to approach their work with a sense of responsibility, ethics, and respect for individuals, in contrast to the manipulative and intrusive nature of modern advertising. It advocates for design that is transparent, unobtrusive, and in the service of the individual's self-determination.
